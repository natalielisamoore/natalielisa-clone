Naming

A visual identity for

Skin to Sky
2024

Alex came to me wanting to create a brand for both of her offerings - merging holistic facials and guided spiritual journey's in the Sedona desert. We began by finding a name that encompasses both of her businesses, and Skin to Sky was born! It's a neat and fun process naming your brand; it takes you on many twists and turns, finally landing on something that feels just right. I then created a custom logo that aligned with that vision. We both agreed this etheric lil nymph represents the feeling she wants women to feel when they enter her world — free and beautiful in their own skin.
