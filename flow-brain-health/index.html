A brand and web design for

Flow Brain Health
2024

I had the pleasure of working closely with Caroline over the past few months to help bring her vision, Flow Brain Health, online. NeurOptimal® is an advanced neurotechnology designed to train the brain to function at peak performance levels. This innovative form of neurofeedback is not a medical treatment, but rather a training for the brain. Together, we worked on her logo, colour palette, copywriting and developed a website that showcases the core information of NeurOptimal that is clear and easy to understand. The finished product is a website that is harmonious, spacious and approachable to a wide range of clients who may be seeking her services.
