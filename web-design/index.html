Why Squarespace?

Built for beautiful, professional websites

Squarespace offers stunning templates designed by award-winning professionals, perfect for showcasing your brand in a visually appealing way. With drag-and-drop functionality and customisable design tools, Squarespace makes it easy to create a beautiful website. However, to truly stand out and maintain a cohesive brand identity, working with an expert designer ensures your website is polished, professional, and optimized for your specific needs.

All-In-One platform for business growth

Squarespace is more than just a website builder—it’s a complete platform with built-in ecommerce, blogging, and appointment scheduling. Its tools allow businesses to expand into new areas effortlessly. A professional designer can further streamline this process, integrating these features in a way that matches your brand’s look and feel while keeping user experience smooth and intuitive.

SEO and Marketing Features at Your Fingertips

With built-in SEO tools, email marketing integration, and social media sharing options, Squarespace helps you grow your online presence. A professional designer can amplify these tools by ensuring your site structure is SEO-friendly, and by creating conversion-optimized landing pages, which helps drive traffic and increase sales.

Secure, Reliable Hosting with 24/7 Support

Squarespace provides secure hosting and around-the-clock customer support, allowing you to focus on running your business without worrying about website maintenance. When you work with a designer, you’re ensuring that your site is not only visually stunning but also built with best practices for performance, security, and mobile responsiveness, keeping your site fast and secure.

How long does it take to build a custom Squarespace website?

The timeline for a custom website varies depending on your needs. On average, a fully customised site can take between 4–6 weeks, from initial consultation to launch. I’ll provide a detailed timeline after we discuss your project scope

How much do services cost?

Pricing depends on the complexity and scope of your project.

Custom websites start at $2500. Website strategy sessions are priced at $350. I offer discounts of you add branding to your package. For a personalized quote, contact me for a free consultation.

Will I be able to update my Squarespace website on my own after it's built?

Absolutely! Squarespace is designed to be user-friendly, and I’ll provide you with guidance after.  However, I’m always available for ongoing maintenance if you prefer.
