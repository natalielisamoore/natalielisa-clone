A cyber altar for

Kandra Alaya
2023

Custom branding and web design project I had the honour of crafting. Kandra's values of devotion, truth, surrender and freedom guided my design process as I captured that essence into a visual signature. This hand drawn logo features a hawk, all-seeing eye and lotus flower; potent symbols that accompanied her spiritual journey. From branding, design + development (in KAJABI) this has been an absolute dream project of mine that has so much love and intention poured into it. I focused on infusing an ethereal aesthetic, spaciousness & heavenly notes, soft, muted colours, occasional earthy elements, elegant script. Each phase of the process was intentional to capture the essence of Kandra’s business. She wanted a cyber altar to host all of her offerings and we achieved just that.
