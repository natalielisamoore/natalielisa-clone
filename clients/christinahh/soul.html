How it works

My voice scans your body, revealing areas where energy is heavy, constricted, or stuck. As I sonically explore these areas, emotions are revealed and mirrored. At the same time, I receive images, symbols, and stories of this emotional material. Sound opens a psychic channel.

Often, the act of mirroring or 'seeing' these old stories, and reflecting them with emotional sound, releases the bound-up energy. It's like discovering a little child that's been ignored, sometimes for generations. This can allow old energy to move — if it's ready.

Preparation

Wear comfortable clothes, have water nearby, and create a space where you can lie down undisturbed. Position your phone or laptop so your camera can 'see' your body.

After your session

Drink lots of water. Be gentle with yourself. Relax. Don't plan big energy-draining activities for the day or evening following your session.
