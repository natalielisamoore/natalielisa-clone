Sound

An ancient, proven portal to calm and well-being

I trained with sound healing pioneer Vickie Dodd beginning in 2015. As an instructor at her Sacred Sound retreats, I led workshops, performances, and sounding sessions. We collaborated on a book: Your Voice is Medicine: Activate the Evolutionary Art of Sound Healing in Your Own Body.

I offer Sound Baths and simple Vocal Sounding techniques online and in-person — simple ways to reset your nervous system and release emotional residue so you can come back home to yourself.

Your body adores
the sound of your own voice

Of all creative arts and healing modalities, making sound soothes tension and releases emotions, quickly. Sounding is not about singing 'nicely' for others. Sounding is making sound medicine for our bodies and spirits. The vibrations of your voice alchemize how you feel. Headaches melt. Confidence grows. Relaxation spreads.

It's as simple as a sigh, a hum, a tone. Or as soulful as singing your own heartsong. So let's dive in, together.
