Singing for Water

I love water. Every day since 2018, I sing for the waters — oceans, rivers, lakes, streams, springs — even puddles. So Mama Earth knows she is loved.

Water is Life. Water amplifies frequencies of sound and intention. Every drop of water eventually touches every other drop. May this Love ripple far and wide.

You are mostly water, darlin.' What does your heart sing for?

Singing Circles
& Vocal Empowerment

Humans feel better when we sing together. Singing boosts endorphins and oxytocin, so we feel more united.

Your voice is the holy path from your heart to the world.

In my vocal workshops and circles, we gently open our voices. Build breath into tones. Tones into rhythm. We share songs we adore, and songs we co-create in the moment.

As a student of Bobby McFerrin (Don't Worry, Be Happy) I share his circle singing practice. I once was too scared to sing on stage with him. No more — it's a story of fear to freedom.

Original Songs

Trained in jazz and gospel vocals, I adore improvising and creating songs in the moment. My songs rely on the naked human voice more than instrumentation. With the Loopy app I compose multi-layered Song Sketches — not as polished as most recordings, but alive with feeling.
