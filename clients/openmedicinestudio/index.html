Project Summary
Open Medicine Studio is a new Salt Spring Island–based medical practice with no existing brand identity, website, or digital presence.

This project covers the creation of both from the ground up — a complete brand identity system and a fully designed and built website ready for launch.

The practice comprises three distinct but integrated programme arms: a community-facing Longitudinal Family Practice, a private Remote Monitoring & AI Collaboration service, and a private Neuroadaptive Intervention Programme.

The direction for this project is clear: simple and clinically elegant. The completed brand will be documented in a full Brand Identity Manual governing all future communications.
