Usage Notes

Excavator Door

  • Use the Colour on Black version (file 02)
  • Vinyl cut or printed decal on black door panel
  • Minimum recommended width: 300mm
  • Keep clear space equal to the height of the wordmark on all sides

Shirts

  • Dark shirts — use Colour on Black or White on Black version
  • Light shirts — use Colour on White or Black on White version
  • Embroidery or screen print both work at this scale
  • Avoid placing on busy or patterned fabric
